How will we get numbers from images? QGIS!

Installing QGIS

1. Go to https://qgis.org/download/

2. Click “Skip it and go to download”, or contribute to the QGIS project

2. Download and install the QGIS Standalone Installer Version 3.34 or later. It may be helpful to download the stable long-term release rather than the other versions.  

3. Open the Installer and click “Next” through the steps, then “Install”

4. Open QGIS.

5. Project → New.

6. File → Save As  (then save)

7. Select tif image or other file of interest and drag them into the blank area in the middle of the project.

Layers Panel: Files you have added will show up in the Layers Panel at the bottom left. Uncheck the box next to them to hide them. Layers shown at the top of the list will cover the other layers in the main part of the screen, “hiding” the lower layers. If you ever zoom too far out, you can right click the layer in the Layers Panel and click “Zoom to Layer” to find it again.
