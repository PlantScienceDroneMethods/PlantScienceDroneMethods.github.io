How to split a field map into plot images

  1. View → Panels → Processing Toolbox

  1. Search “Clip Raster by Mask Layer”

  1. Plug in information into the tool:
  1. Your raster (map, DSM, etc)
  2. Your grid geopackage
  3. Make sure the iterate symbol is checked  
  4. Consider checking boxes with other output options, as shown below,
  5. To the right of “Clipped mask”, click the three dots and specify an output folder and base name.

  1. Click Run, and the files will be exported to the directory you specified!
